# Overview

Q-SYS Reflect offers a remote management and monitoring solution for AV systems. It leverages Q-SYS technology to provide visibility and control of all AV devices (including native and third party devices) across your estate in real-time, securely, from anywhere in the world. It can also connect to other IT platforms to enable your AV data to add value to those other IT systems for far deeper, actionable insights across your enterprise.

## Organizations, Domains, Systems, and Peripherals

Q-SYS Reflect uses a hierarchy of Organizations, Domains, and Systems (which in turn can have connected Peripherals).

* An Organization represents the end-user, typically a company name. It contains one or more Domains.
* A Domain typically represents a physical location within an Organization. It contains one or more Systems.
* A System is a single Q-SYS design file running on a Q-SYS core processor or redundant pair of Cores. The design file contains connections to Peripherals. A System could represent a particular space, such as a conference room, within the Domain.
* A Peripheral (monitored) is any Q-SYS or third-party device monitored by Q-SYS Reflect, such as amplifiers, cameras, touch screens, and network video endpoints.

**Note:** Q-SYS peripheral devices are automatically monitored by Q-SYS Reflect. Monitoring of third-party devices is optional.

## Q-SYS Reflect Subscriptions

Q-SYS Reflect is a subscription service. When you subscribe, you specify the quantity of Systems for monitoring, as well as a Software Tier, which determines what features are available.

**Tip:** Visit the [Q-SYS Reflect](https://www.qsys.com/products/q-sys/monitoring-management/q-sys-reflect/) product page for pricing information.

Q-SYS Reflect uses a tiered subscription model:

* Q-SYS Reflect is a free subscription that enables powerful monitoring features for your Q-SYS systems.
* Q-SYS Reflect Plus subscriptions add enterprise-level control and management capabilities, such as remote UCI access and remote Q-SYS Designer.
