# Q-SYS Library

Q-SYS Library is a one-stop hub for verified, ready-to-use assets such as Q-SYS plugins, UCI templates, and sample designs.

A Q-SYS Verified Asset can be a plugin, CSS file, UCI template, Sample Design, and/or Lua Module that has gone through rigorous testing and review for a solution that is recommended alongside the Q-SYS platform and Q-SYS products. The solution may be for a third party product or service but can also be used as a standalone utility or template for use inside of Q-SYS Designer. All Q-SYS Verified Assets undergo a review against our standards for verification and Reflect Enablement, when applicable. The assets are supported by us and our partners and are held to our highest quality and best-practice standards.

**Note:** Q-SYS Designer Software v10.1 or higher is required to use Asset Installer.
