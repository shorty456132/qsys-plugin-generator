# Video > Cameras

Use the Cameras page to view and set Privacy and Offline images for all Q-SYS cameras in your design. At least one Q-SYS camera must be present in the running design for these settings to appear.

**Note:** Q-SYS Designer Software versions 9.7.0 and later include high resolution default images for Privacy, Exiting Privacy, and Offline, as well as support for high resolution uploaded images. If your pre-9.7.0 design currently uses default images, they will be updated automatically in 9.7.0 and later. Custom, uploaded images are retained when upgrading to 9.7.0 and later. However, in most cases, re-uploading custom images will greatly improve quality.

**Tip:** Camera image files are stored and updated within the running design file. Load and save the design in Q-SYS Designer Software to capture any updates to image files.

## Images

Configure the JPG images to appear during various camera states. Click the  icon to select a JPG file with a maximum size of 3 MB. Images with a 16:9 aspect ratio are recommended.

#### Privacy Image

This image appears when the camera is in Privacy Mode.

#### Exiting Privacy Image

This image appears when the camera is transitioning out of Privacy Mode.

#### Offline Image

This image appears when the camera is offline.
