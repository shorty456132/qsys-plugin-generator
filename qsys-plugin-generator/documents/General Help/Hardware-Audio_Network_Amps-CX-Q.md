# CX-Q Series Network Amplifiers

CX-Q Series network amplifiers combine a legacy of robust power amplifiers, advancements in high-efficiency output devices, and native network transport, control and monitoring capabilities of the Q-SYS Ecosystem. CX-Q Series features four-and eight-channel models, capable of delivering customized power output loading and a total maximum power of up to 8000 W. Low impedance, 70 V or 100 V direct drive are available on all channels.

**Note:** This topic provides an overview of CX-Q amplifiers. For installation instructions and other documentation, see the [CX-Q Series](https://www.qsys.com/systems/products/q-sys-platform/products-peripherals-accessories/network-amplifiers/cx-q-series/) product page on the QSC website.
