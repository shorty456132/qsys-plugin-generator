# SIP Conferencing Integration

Use these topics as a starting point for integrating Q-SYS Softphone with third-party telephony and video telepresence systems and devices. Due to the nature of phone systems and related protocols (SIP, SDP, DTMF, etc.), always apply extra time for checking documents from the third-party system provider to understand if they support standard SIP phones such as Q-SYS Softphone.
