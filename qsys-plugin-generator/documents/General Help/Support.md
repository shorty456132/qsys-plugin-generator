### Remote Support Portal

Q-SYS Support uses TeamViewer for a simple and secure remote support application. A high-speed internet connection is required.

1. Click [here](https://software.qsc.com/qsys/QSC_Remote_Support.exe) to download the Remote Support standalone executable to your computer.
2. Double-click the executable to run the TeamViewer app. (No installation is required.)
3. Call us, and then provide the Support person the unique ID number.
