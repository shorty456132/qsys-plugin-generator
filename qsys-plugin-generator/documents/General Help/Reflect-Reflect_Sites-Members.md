# Members

The Members page provides a listing of all Domain members, their roles, and level of access to the Q-SYS Cores within the Domain. Use this section to invite new members and assign Domain and Core Access roles.

## Invite a New Member

1. Click + Invite Member.
2. Type the Email Address of the invitee.
3. Select a Role. For more information, see [Reflect Users and Roles](Users_Roles.htm#Site).
4. Optionally type a Custom Message for the invitee (up to 500 characters), and then click Send Invite.

The invite now appears in the [Invites](#Invites) tab.

## Manage Members

[Active](javascript:void(0))

The Active tab lists all the current members of the Domain. If you are member designated as a Domain Manager, you can:

* Click Edit to modify any member's Domain Role and Core Access level. For more information, see [Reflect Users and Roles](Users_Roles.htm#Site).
* Click Remove to delete the member from the Domain.

**Note:** Members whose role is "Member" can only view the list of current Domain members.

[Invites](javascript:void(0))

The Invites tab lists all open invitations to the Domain. Invites remain in Pending status until the individual accepts the invitation from the email. If you are a member designated as a Domain Manager, you can:

* Click Edit to modify the invitee's Role.
* Click Resend Invite to send another invitation email â for example, if the individual did not receive the original invite.
* Click Cancel Invite to rescind the invitation.

  **Note:** When you cancel an invite, the individual does not receive an email indicating the cancellation. Rather, the "Accept Invitation" button within the original invitation email links to a "Invitation Link Expired" page.
